Features

Avail Tax Benefits

Health Insurance Policy not only covers your medical expenses but also saves you money on taxes. As Medical Insurance is an essential investment, Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961 allows a tax deduction for the premium paid towards a Medical Insurance plan. Here are a few additional advantages.

$
Tax Deduction for Paying the Premium

You can claim tax deductions of up to Rs. 25,000/- by paying a Health Insurance premium for yourself or your family. If you are paying the premium for your parents as well, you can claim a higher tax deduction of up to Rs. 1 lakh.

$
Tax Deduction for Health Check-Up

In addition to premiums, you can also claim tax deductions for expenses incurred towards preventive health check-ups as well. Under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961, you can claim Income Tax deductions up to Rs. 5,000/-.
